Join The Lab

We are always looking for highly motivated students to join our team. Our research is multi-disciplinary, drawing on expertise in control theory, robotics, and machine learning. If you are excited about solving challenging problems in robotics and autonomy, we encourage you to apply.

Prospective PhD Students

Ideal candidates have a strong background in one or more of control systems, dynamics, optimization, machine learning or robotics. Proficiency in programming (Python, C++, MATLAB) and experience with hardware or simulation platforms is highly desirable.

To apply, please submit your application directly to the NYU's ECE PhD program and mention Prof. Khorrami's name in your statement of purpose.

Master's and Undergraduate Students

We welcome current students at NYU to get involved in our research. Opportunities are available for master's theses, independent study projects, and undergraduate research programs. A strong academic record in relevant coursework is required. Please email Prof. Khorrami with your transcript, resume, and a brief statement of your research interests.

Postdoctoral Fellows

We periodically have openings for postdoctoral researchers. Candidates with a strong publication record in top-tier robotics or control conferences and journals are encouraged to apply. To inquire about available positions, please email Prof. Khorrami directly with your CV and research statement.
