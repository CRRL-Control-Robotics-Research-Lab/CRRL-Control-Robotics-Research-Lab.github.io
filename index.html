About Us

Our lab focuses on cutting-edge research in robotics, control, and dynamic systems. We combine theory and practice to develop intelligent robotic systems for real-world applications.

Research Directions

Nonlinear Control and Large Scale Systems

We develop advanced nonlinear control strategies to ensure stability and performance for complex, large-scale dynamic systems, including robotic networks and power grids.

Robotics and Automation

Our research covers the full spectrum of robotics, from legged locomotion and dexterous manipulation to the automation of complex tasks in manufacturing and logistics.

Unmanned Autonomous Vehicles

We focus on the guidance, navigation, and control of unmanned aerial (UAVs) and ground (UGVs) vehicles, with an emphasis on autonomous operation in challenging environments.

Multi-Agent Systems

Our work explores decentralized coordination and cooperative control of robot swarms and multi-agent networks for tasks such as surveillance, exploration, and collaborative transport.

Machine Learning Applications

We apply machine learning, particularly reinforcement learning and computer vision, to enhance robot autonomy, improve control performance, and detect threats in secure systems.

Cyber-Physical Systems Security

We investigate the security of cyber-physical systems, developing methods to detect, prevent, and mitigate attacks on critical infrastructure and autonomous robotic platforms.
